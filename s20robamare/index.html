Factorio

Factorio is a game in which you build and maintain factories. You will be mining resources, researching technologies, building infrastructure, automating production and fighting enemies. It is a game about automation, industriousness, imperialism, and the effects of excessive sleep deprevation on the body and mind.

You play a man known as The Engineer who has crash landed on an alien planet with strange trees, lakes filled with fish and lots of resources. But on this alien planet are... aliens, a lot of agressive hippies that do not like pollution, but simultaneously they feed of of it to become bigger, stronger and much more vicious. So just like normal hippies.
